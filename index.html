Give your documents real powers?

If you want your ebooks, brochures, and reports to make a difference online, make them different. Beautiful, easy to share, and insightful.

Excellent look on any device

Your PDF transforms into an HTML5 flipbook with videos, interactive navigation and custom design. It’s more of a website than a file.

Share it in any way you want

Host your document online, get its link and share it anywhere: send by email, embed to website, post to social media.

Add Interaction

Make your publications more engaging with built-in YouTube, Vimeo, and Wistia videos. The videos will add drive and value to the content in your documents, and embedding them is simple – just paste a video link anywhere on the page.

Interactive accessibility

To make sure your content is available to anyone and on any device, simply create an accessible PDF version of your publication. This way, clients will be able to open, download, and interact with your content on screen readers, desktops, and mobile devices alike.

Content protection

Control who can share, print, and download your digital publications to avoid unwanted viewing. You can also password protect your content and restrict the websites it can be viewed on.

Google Analytics integration

Google Analytics will show you how your publications are performing. Track interactions with your content and learn more about your audience.

  • Sharing Control
  • Video and link adding
  • Logo display
  • Google Analytics
  • PDF download button
  • Password protected (if required)
  • Monthly google reports
